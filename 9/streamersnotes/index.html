So you’ve decided to be a part of the show, but how does it work?

Remote Segments:

  1. Grab our overlay and add it into your OBS/streaming software as a Source > Browser:

https://obs.donations.generozity.com.au/generozity-9

  1. Know where people can go to donate! (add it as a link, in chatbot, whatever)

https://donate.generozity.charity/

  1. Before your segment, jump into our Discord and head into the room “STUDIO”

A member of the team will be there to chat!

  1. 5/10 minutes before your segment starts, start up streaming with a “starting soon” scene etc. When it’s time to begin, our team member in Discord will say “okay sweet, cutting to you” and you start streaming to your channel AS USUAL


How it works is we take that feed and mirror it on twitch.tv/GenerOZity 

(you can also stream directly to us, it’s just as easy, but it means you don’t get to go live on your channel too)

If you need help with any of this, let us know, we are here to help!

Physical Segments:

  1. Rock up to the venue 30 minutes or more before your segment
  2. Be greeted by one of the team, get a drink, maybe a snack, say hey to your friends and some new people

  1. Do some socials, go live on your own channel if you want and tell them you’re about to do your segment

  1. Jump behind one of the PCs or consoles (our team will have gotten your game all ready to go)
  2. Help raise money for Cure Cancer & Hang out afterwards and have fun

SOME THINGS TO THINK ABOUT

Donation incentives: They are really good, they are usually the driving factor to how much money raised in each segment.

If you’re looking for some fun ideas, here are some of the classics that always seem to do well:

  • Eat something hot (hot sauce, chillies, whatever)
  • Nerf gun to the face
  • Pie in the face (it’s a classic)
  • Shaving off eyebrows, beards, hair, generally shaving anything that won’t break TOS
  • In-game incentives (helping or hurting you able to play the game as it’s intended, like wearing boxing gloves)
  • Pouring a giant bucket of slime over your head

COVID Safety: For this show, we’re asking everyone who is doing a physical segment AND THEN WANTING TO HANG OUT AFTERWARDS to do a covid test - That means getting a test then isolate while waiting for their results then travel directly to the venue (wearing masks etc)

You’ll be greeted with hand sanitizer and a QR code to sign in but with all that being said, make sure someone is cool with you walking up and giving them a hug before you do it.

We will also be cleaning all peripherals + controllers, but if you want to bring your own that’s also totally fine.

We’re trying to create what we are calling the “GenerOZity bubble” where everyone can be comfortable doing their thing and not worrying about who last touched a controller etc.
